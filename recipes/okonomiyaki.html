Okonomiyaki

Description

A popular street food from Osaka, Okonomiyaki is a delicious Japanese savory pancake “grilled as you like it“ with your choice of protein and tasty condiments and toppings. My recipe includes the 6 key ingredients that give your Okonomiyaki a truly authentic taste.

Ingredients

For the batter:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 5.6 oz nagaimo
  • 3/4 cup dashi
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup tenkasu
  • 1/4 cup pickled red ginger
For the sauce:
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 3 1/2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine 1 cup flour, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp sugar, and 1/4 tsp baking powder.
  2. Peel and grate 5.6 oz nagaimo in a small bowl.
  3. Add the grated nagaimo and 3/4 cup dashi to the bowl.
  4. Mix it all together until combined. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it rest in the refrigerator for at least one hour. Meanwhile, prepare the sauce.
  5. Combine 1 1/2 Tbsp sugar, 2 Tbsp oyster sauce, 1/4 cup ketchup, and 3 1/2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce in a small bowl. Mix together until the sugar is dissolved.
  6. After one hour, take out the batter from the refrigerator. Add 4 large eggs (50 g each w/o shell), ½ cup tenkasu/agedama (tempura scraps), and ¼ cup pickled red ginger (beni shoga or kizami beni shoga) to the bowl. Mix until combined.
  7. Add the minced cabbage to the batter, one-third of it at a time. Mix well before adding the rest.
  8. In a large pan, heat a bit of neutral oil on medium heat. When the frying pan is hot (400ºF or 200ºC), spread the batter in a circle on the pan.
  9. Place 2–3 slices of pork belly on top of the okonomiyaki and cook covered for 5 minutes.
  10. When the bottom side is nicely browned, flip it over.
  11. Gently press the okonomiyaki to fix its shape and keep it together. Cover and cook for another 5 minutes.
  12. Flip it over one last time and cook uncovered for 2 minutes. If you’re going to cook the next batch, transfer the cooked okonomiyaki to a plate. Spread sauce on top with a brush or spoon.
